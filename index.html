SRM Machine Intelligence Community

We are an undergraduate research community focusing our efforts on the latest happenings in the field of machine intelligence. Producing impactful projects and gaining meaningful insights stays a priority in our operation.

What we do

Student-led Research

We are a community of students aimed at promoting and fostering the growing interest around machine intelligence on campus. We currently hold weekly discussions on the latest papers in the field, organize crash courses, host speakers, and arrange competitions around machine intelligence at SRM.

Reading Groups

ML and DL are fields where theory is one of the keys to having a powerful basis and understanding of the code as well as learning new ways to implement it. At MIC, we hold reading sessions by weekly on research papers for staying up-to-date with the fast, ever-growing field of ML and DL.

Projects

Projects are the perfect way to check your own understanding of the theory and code. We at MIC, lead projects to learn as well as implement what we've learned to solve real world problems to foster experience based learning.

Workshops

Workshops are a way to bridge the gap between theory and experience. At MIC, we hold workshops to provide a practical learning experience for the complicated theory that follows this field.
